What is Alquilando?



Alquilando is a digital marketplace based in Buenos Aires that integrates the services of brokers, insurers and banks to offer a customized and simplified solution to renting in Argentina. It was the recipient of the Best Startup in Argentina and the Most Time-Savior Startup of the world in the SeedStars 2018 competition as well as the 2018 BBVA award for Open Innovation in Argentina. Below are descriptions of a few projects I was able to develop for Alquilando during my time in Argentina.



Centralized Dashboards for Visualization and Analysis of Ad Campaign Data

Contributed to the design and development of several interactive and customizable dashboards for analysis that integrate Alquilando's ad and marketing campaign data streams with Google Data Studio and Cyfe.



Key Features
1) Integrates real-time data from Google and Facebook Ad Campaigns
2) Allows for filtering by campaign titles, types, or keywords.
3) Presents geographical breakdown and analysis of campaigns.
4) Customizable date intervals to view trends in different periods.
5) Notification alerts when key values rise or fall past set limit.

Automation of Contract Generation and Processing

The Alquilando Contract Generator is a web-based application that automates the creation and distribution of Alquilando contracts, reducing the original 25 minute length of the process by 80%.



Key Features
1) Automized calculation of monetary formulas and date intervals.
2) Adheres to variable legal format requirements and restrictions.
3) Toggle between optional subclauses and cases.
4) Customizable for six different types of contract.
5) Conversion of currencies and of numbers to written words.
6) Supported in English and Spanish.

Comparative Market Monitor

The Alquilando Market Report Generator routinely scrapes and analyzes market data in five target cities (Buenos Aires, Argentina; Santiago, Chile; Miami, Florida; Bogota, Colombia; and Mexico City, Mexico) to generate comparative reports for analysis, including information on leading competitors, REITs (FIBRAs), family offices, potential investors and general real estate market trends and population statistics.



Key Features
1) Generates graphical comparisons from key data scraped from multiple databases.
2) Interprets collected data and generates written analyses in Spanish and English.
3) Customizable to report on selected cities or time periods at specified time intervals.
4) Customizable output format.
