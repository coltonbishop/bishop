Wengga is a web platform for the crowd-sourced active learning and machine translation of resource-scarce and indigenous language data. Its goal is to empower communities to collect their own data and create their own translators. The platform prompts users to translate critical sentences (according to a perplexity-based active learning algorithm), and the translators Wengga generates from this data are powered by an attention-based sequence-to-sequence neural network optimized for low-resource machine translation.
