TigerTales is an online marketplace for Princeton students looking to buy and sell textbooks. TigerTales is a CAS authenticated website which allows students to easily search for the textbooks they need by entering the course they are searching for. They can also choose to be notified immediately by email when a book they are searching for is posted to the market.
